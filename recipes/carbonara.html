Carbonara

Pasta alla Carbonara is a legendary Roman dish celebrated all over the world. It represents a major highlight of central Italian cuisine. The recipe combines eggs, guanciale, pecorino, and black pepper to create an incredibly creamy and unmistakable flavor. It pairs perfectly with classic pasta shapes like spaghetti or rigatoni.

Ingredients

  • 320g of pasta
  • Eggs
  • Guanciale
  • Pepper
  • Pecorino Romano

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta.
  2. Crisp the guanciale in a pan along with the pepper.
  3. Drain the pasta directly into the pan with the guanciale.
  4. Toss and cream everything together off the heat, adding egg yolks and pecorino Romano.
  5. Plate the dish and top with another grating of pecorino cheese
